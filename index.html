ABOUT


This work was inspired by the Google Experiment made by Iacopo Sassarini in 2011.

The Hopalong Attractor algorithm developed by Barry Martin is used to to generate fractal point maps.

The visualizer then renders these maps in 3D space using THREE.js


CONTROLS


  • [Mouse Move] Change camera position
  • [F] Fix camera position

  • [Up-Down] Change particle velocity
  • [Left-Right] Change rotation speed

  • [H] Toggle info and cursor
  • [F11] Toggle fullscreen
